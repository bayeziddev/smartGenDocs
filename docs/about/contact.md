# Contact Us: Get in Touch with the SmartGen Docs Team

We value your feedback, questions, and collaboration. Whether you need support, have a business inquiry, or want to explore partnership opportunities, there are several ways to get in touch with the SmartGen Docs team. Please choose the most appropriate channel for your needs to ensure a timely and effective response.

## 1. Community Support and General Inquiries

For technical support, questions about using SmartGen Docs, or general discussions, we encourage you to utilize our community channels. This allows other community members to benefit from the answers and contributes to a growing knowledge base.

*   **GitHub Discussions**: This is the primary place for asking questions, sharing ideas, and engaging in broader conversations about SmartGen Docs.
    *   **Purpose**: Technical support, feature ideas, usage questions, general Q&A.
    *   **Link**: [SmartGen Docs GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/bayeziddev/smartGenDocs/discussions)
    *   **Best Practice**: Search existing discussions before posting. Provide clear context and details in your post.

*   **Real-time Chat**: For quick questions and informal interactions, join our community chat.
    *   **Purpose**: Immediate help, quick clarifications, networking.
    *   **Link**: [Check the SmartGen Docs GitHub Repository](https://github.com/bayeziddev/smartGenDocs) or the official SmartGen Platform website for the latest invite link (e.g., Discord or Slack).
    *   **Best Practice**: Keep questions concise. For complex issues, you may be directed to GitHub Discussions or Issues.

## 2. Bug Reports and Feature Requests

If you encounter a bug or have a specific feature you'd like to see implemented, please use our GitHub Issues tracker.

*   **GitHub Issues**: The official channel for reporting bugs and submitting formal feature requests.
    *   **Purpose**: Documenting reproducible bugs, proposing new functionalities.
    *   **Link**: [SmartGen Docs GitHub Issues](https://github.com/bayeziddev/smartGenDocs/issues)
    *   **Best Practice**: Follow the guidelines in our [Reporting Issues](../community/issues.md) guide to provide all necessary information.

## 3. Business Inquiries and Partnerships

For business-related matters, potential collaborations, or partnership opportunities, please reach out via email.

*   **Email**: `contact@smartgentools.com`
    *   **Purpose**: Partnership proposals, business development, sponsorship inquiries, large-scale adoption discussions.
    *   **Best Practice**: Provide a clear subject line and detailed information about your inquiry to help us direct it to the right team member.

## 4. Media and Press Inquiries

Members of the press or media seeking information, interviews, or official statements regarding SmartGen Docs or the SmartGen Platform should contact us directly via email.

*   **Email**: `media@smartgentools.com`
    *   **Purpose**: Press releases, interview requests, media kits, official statements.
    *   **Best Practice**: Clearly state your publication/organization and the nature of your inquiry.

## 5. General Feedback

For any other feedback, suggestions, or comments that don't fit into the above categories, you can use the general contact email.

*   **Email**: `feedback@smartgentools.com`
    *   **Purpose**: General suggestions, website feedback, non-urgent comments.

We look forward to hearing from you and appreciate your interest in SmartGen Docs!
